Sync vs. Async

Synchronous (Blocking)

You wait at the restaurant for your pizza to be cooked. You can't do anything else.

Asynchronous (Non-blocking)

You order pizza, go do laundry, and the delivery guy rings the doorbell when it's ready.

Consensus: Paxos & Raft

Imagine a room of people trying to agree on a pizza topping. Consensus algorithms (like Paxos or Raft) are the rules they use to agree, even if some people are shouting, can't hear, or leave the room.

  • Leader Election (Who orders?)
  • Log Replication (Everyone writes down the same order)
  • Safety (No two people order different pizzas)
