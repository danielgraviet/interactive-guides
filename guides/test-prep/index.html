15-Minute Pre-Test
Study Plan

Anchored in faith · Guided by the Spirit

0–2 min
🙏 Opening Prayer
Doctrine & Covenants 9:8

Begin with a sincere, brief prayer. Ask Heavenly Father to calm your mind, sharpen your memory, and help the Spirit guide your thoughts. Express gratitude for the opportunity to learn.

"You must study it out in your mind; then you must ask me if it be right…"

D&C 9:8

Remember: you've done the studying. Now invite the Spirit to be your companion through the test.

2–5 min
📖 A Calming Scripture
Joshua 1:9 · Philippians 4:13

Read one or two short scriptures slowly. Let the words sink in. Don't rush — feel the reassurance they offer.

"Be strong and of good courage; be not afraid, neither be thou dismayed: for the Lord thy God is with thee whithersoever thou goest."

Joshua 1:9

"I can do all things through Christ which strengtheneth me."

Philippians 4:13
5–10 min
🧠 Quick Review
D&C 88:118

Spend five focused minutes reviewing your most important notes — key terms, formulas, or concepts. Keep it light and confidence-building, not stress-inducing.

  • Glance at chapter headings or your summary sheet
  • Recite 3–5 key facts aloud from memory
  • If something feels fuzzy, breathe and move on — trust your preparation

"Seek ye out of the best books words of wisdom; seek learning, even by study and also by faith."

D&C 88:118
10–13 min
🌬️ Breathe & Center
Alma 37:37 · Isaiah 26:3

Set your notes aside. Place your feet flat on the floor. Do three slow, deep breaths — inhale for 4 counts, hold for 4, exhale for 6. Let your shoulders drop.

Silently repeat a short affirmation rooted in faith:

"Counsel with the Lord in all thy doings… and he will direct thee for good."

Alma 37:37

Picture yourself walking calmly into the test, mind clear, Spirit present.

13–15 min
✨ Closing Affirmation & Go
2 Nephi 4:35 · D&C 6:36

Offer a brief, grateful closing prayer. Thank Heavenly Father for your mind, your teachers, and your preparation. Ask for peace and confidence to carry with you.

"Look unto me in every thought; doubt not, fear not."

D&C 6:36

Stand up, take one more breath, and go forward with faith. You are ready.

"For God hath not given us the spirit of fear;
but of power, and of love, and of a sound mind."

2 Timothy 1:7
